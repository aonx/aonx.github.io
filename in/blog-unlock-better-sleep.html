Sleep is more than just rest—it's a powerful recovery tool. By understanding the types of sleep and tracking them with tools like the Ziva Ring, you can take control of your health and energy levels.

The Types of Sleep

Sleep occurs in cycles, each with unique benefits:
  • Light Sleep: The gateway to deeper rest, helping your body wind down.
  • Deep Sleep: Crucial for physical recovery and immune function.
  • REM Sleep: Where dreaming happens, vital for memory and emotional health.

Each stage plays a role in how refreshed and energized you feel each day.

How Ziva Ring Tracks Your Sleep

The Ziva Ring uses advanced sensors to monitor:
  • Sleep Stages: Breakdowns of light, deep, and REM sleep.
  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV): A key recovery indicator.
  • Body Temperature & Movement: For insights into sleep quality.
