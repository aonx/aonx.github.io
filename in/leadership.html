Our Advisor

Dr Riyas Segu Mohamed

Wellness

Dr. Riyas Segu Mohamed serves as the Wellness Advisor for the ZivaOne Smart Ring, bringing expertise in healthcare innovation, preventive care, and holistic wellness. With a background in clinical research and digital health, he guides the integration of medical science into the ring’s wellness ecosystem.

At ZivaOne, Dr. Riyas ensures that every feature—from stress monitoring to sleep tracking—is grounded in evidence-based practices. His insights help us translate complex health data into meaningful, actionable guidance that supports healthier lifestyles. With his direction, the ZivaOne Smart Ring becomes a true personal wellness companion—backed by science and built for everyday life.
