Privacy policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting how your personal information is handled. Our Privacy Policy outlines how we safeguard your privacy as a user (“you,” “your,” “user(s)”) when you visit this website, along with any other websites, mobile or digital applications, or services we may offer that link to this Privacy Policy (collectively referred to as the “Platform”). The Privacy Policy details the types of information we collect and monitor when you use our Platform, as well as how this information is used and shared.

Your consent:

By accessing or using the ZivaOne application, including downloading the mobile app, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy, or as otherwise required or permitted by applicable law.

Scope of this Privacy Policy:

This Privacy Policy addresses the collection of personally identifiable information ("personal information") when you access or use our application. It does not apply to the policies or practices of companies we do not own or control, or to individuals we do not employ.

Personal information refers to details that identify, relate to, describe, or can reasonably be linked to you, either on its own or combined with other information we have. Examples of personal information include your name, contact details, and any information you provide when creating an account or profile on our application.

We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from individuals under the age of 18, nor do we allow such persons to register for our products or services, including personalized fitness or nutrition plans, product delivery, or feedback on activities within the application.

Information that is collected through our application:

We collect the personal information you voluntarily provide when you use our application. Upon sign-up: When you create a user profile on our Platform, we gather your name, age, gender, email address, and any other relevant information you provide. You may also choose to share additional details such as payment information, physical and mental health conditions, health data, location data, nutrition details, dietary and lifestyle information, height, weight, body measurements, passwords, workout data, or other health-related insights relevant to our services and products.

While using our application: As you download and use our mobile application, we may collect information about your mobile device (including device and application identifiers), the referring web page, pages visited, IP address, and location (depending on the permissions you have granted). When we collect usage data from the application, it is used only in aggregate form and not in a way that identifies you personally.

Use of our smart ring: If you use our smart ring, you acknowledge that any information, data, or readings from the smart ring will be automatically uploaded to your user account with us. If these automatically collected readings can identify you personally, they are considered personal information under this Privacy Policy.

Changes to this policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to address changes in the law, our data collection and usage practices, service features, or technological advancements. Your continued use of the Platform, services, and products after any updates to this Privacy Policy will indicate your acceptance and consent to those changes.
