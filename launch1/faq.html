Frequently Asked Questions

Informational Questions:

1. What is a smart ring?
A smart ring is a wearable electronic device with advanced features useful in everyday life.
2. How does the Ziva smart ring work?
The Ziva smart ring connects to your mobile device via Bluetooth and Location. It can display notifications and track physical activity.
3. What is the battery life of the Ziva smart ring?
The battery lasts up to 7 days, depending on usage.
4. Where can I buy the Ziva smart ring?
You can purchase it directly from our website.
5. Which devices and operating systems (OS) is Ziva compatible with?
Currently, the app supports Android and IOS devices. Tablet and iPad support is coming soon.
6. What health metrics does the ring capture?
The Ziva application displays health metrics such as heart rate, SpO2, sleep, step count, calories burned, and heart rate variations. Additional health metrics will be included soon.
7. How do I activate my smart ring before usage?
Ensure your smart ring is fully charged before its first use.. It helps to optimize battery life and ensures the ring functions correctly from the start.
8. How long does it take to charge the smart ring fully?
It takes approximately 2 hours to fully charge your smart ring. Make sure to charge it completely before its first use to ensure optimal performance.

Functional Questions:

1. Is the Ziva smart ring waterproof?
Yes, the smart ring is waterproof up to a certain depth. Check the product specifications for details.
2. How do I charge my Ziva smart ring?
Use the charging dock. Place the ring on the dock and connect the dock to a power source.
3. Can I use the Ziva smart ring without a smartphone?
The Ziva smart ring needs a smartphone for full functionality but can track physical activity without one.
4. Is the Ziva smart ring comfortable to wear?
Yes, it is lightweight and made with hypoallergenic materials.
5. How do I pair my Ziva smart ring with my phone?
Download the Ziva app from the App Store or Google Play Store and follow the prompts to connect your ring.
6. How do I find the right ring size?
Please refer to our size chart to choose the correct size.
7. Can the app be used in landscape mode?
Landscape mode support is coming soon.
